Privacy Policy

Umench ("we", "us", or "our") operates the iOS application "UME Mix" (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically, none of the following information is sent to any external server:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Tracking identifiers such as IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers)
  • Location information
  • System information such as contacts, photos, or calendar
  • Usage analytics, operation logs, or crash reports
  • Playback history, playlists, or imported audio files

All data used in the App (imported audio files, playlists, pomodoro history, settings) is stored only on your device and on the cloud storage you choose (iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.).

2. On-Device Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Audio source references (security-scoped bookmarks)
  • Track metadata extracted from audio files (title, artist, album, artwork, etc.)
  • Playlists
  • Pomodoro session history and category settings
  • Playback cache (temporary local copies of audio files for offline playback)
  • App settings (volume, cache limit, sort order, etc.)

This data is removed when the App is uninstalled. Original files on your cloud storage are unaffected.

3. Network Communication

The App does not perform any network communication other than the following:

  • Downloading audio files from cloud storage you specify (iCloud Drive, etc.) via standard system APIs

The App does not perform any advertising, analytics, or third-party API communication.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your information with any third parties.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following OS permissions as needed. Core functions of the App remain usable even if permissions are declined (with the corresponding feature disabled).

Permission Purpose
File and folder access Reading audio files and folders you select
Local notifications Notifying pomodoro phase completion
Background audio Continuing playback while the device is locked or other apps are in use

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because the App does not collect personal information, it is safe to use at any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect legal or feature changes. We will notify users of material changes through the App or our support page.
